Questions

Does it actually block apps?+

Yes. When you open a blocked app, Broc's wall appears over it and stays until your habits are done. There is no snooze and no "5 more minutes."

What does it cost?+

Broc is a subscription with a 7-day free trial. One plan, everything in: unlimited habits, custom unlock durations, AI photo verification, and full history. No ads, ever.

What does Broc see on my phone?+

Only which app is open right now, so it can compare against your block list. No keylogging, no screen recording, no browsing history. Details in the privacy policy.

Can't I just uninstall it?+

You can. Broc isn't a parental-control cage, it's a wall you chose to build. It makes the lazy path annoying and the honest path rewarding. That's enough for most people, most days.

Which habits work with it?+

Anything: reading, the gym, meditation, cleaning, studying, walking the dog. Photo verification and timers cover most of them; you decide per habit.
