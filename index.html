Questions

Does it actually block apps?

Yes. When you open a blocked app, Broc's lock screen appears over it and stays until your habits are done. There is no snooze and no "5 more minutes."

Is it free?

Yes, with no ads. Broc Pro adds unlimited habits, custom unlock durations, AI photo verification, and full history, with a 7-day free trial.

What does Broc see on my phone?

Only which app is open right now, so it can compare against your block list. No keylogging, no screen recording, no browsing history. Details in the privacy policy.
