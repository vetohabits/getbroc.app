Unrot alternative for Android: Broc

Love the earn-your-screen-time loop, but use Android? Broc brings a similar habit-to-access trade to Android.

By Shay Gavan · Published 12 July 2026 · Reviewed 14 July 2026

Important: Broc is independent and is not made by or affiliated with Unrot. This page compares the public product descriptions so Android users can choose the closest fit.

Is Unrot available on Android?

The current Unrot: Earn Your Screen Time listing is labelled Only for iPhone. If you are on Android and want the same basic bargain—do something useful, then earn access to the apps that pull you in—Broc is built for that gap.

There are other Android apps with “Unrot” in their name. They are not automatically the same product or developer. Check the developer and the mechanic before treating a listing as the iPhone Unrot.

Why people look for an Unrot alternative

Unrot turns screen time into a reward. A walk, study session, reading, cleaning or workout earns credits/coins; those credits unlock the apps you chose. The point is not to delete the apps forever. It is to put the thing you meant to do first.

That is a different category from a normal timer. The reward is connected to a real-world action, so the user is not merely waiting for a countdown to finish.

  • Choose Broc if the habit-to-screen-time trade is the part of Unrot you want and Android is your platform.

Common questions

Is Broc the official Android version of Unrot?

No. Broc is an independent Android app. It is positioned as an alternative for people who want a similar core loop, not as an official port.

Does Broc work exactly like Unrot?

No. The shared idea is “complete something useful, then earn access.” Broc uses direct minute unlocks, Android app blocking, timers and optional AI photo verification; Unrot uses its own iPhone interface and credit system.

Can I use Broc without deleting my social apps?

Yes. Broc is designed to keep the apps installed while the lock screen controls access to the apps you select.

What does Broc cost?

Broc has a 7-day free trial, then a subscription with unlimited habits, custom unlock durations, AI photo verification and history. The current plan is shown in Google Play.
