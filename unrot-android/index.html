Unrot for Android: what's actually available

Short version: two different products have used the name. The habit app appears iPhone-only; a separate Android app is now branded Untap.

By Shay Gavan · Published 12 July 2026 · Reviewed 14 July 2026

Is Unrot on Android?

The answer depends on which product you mean. Unrot: Earn your Screen Time, the habit-and-brain-points app by Unrot OÜ, appears to be iPhone-only: its website currently links to the App Store.

A separate site, unrotapp.com, links to an Android app now branded Untap: Screen Time Control. It is a different developer and a different friction/timer product, not the habit-coin app.

That leaves a real gap. Unrot blew up on TikTok for a reason: it made screen time something you earn. If you watched those videos on an Android phone, this page is for you.

What made Unrot work

Unrot's deal is simple. Real-life habits (a walk, reading, homework, cleaning) earn coins. Coins unlock TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, whatever you've locked. Habits are proven with a timer or a photo snap, a little buddy grows when you follow through, and streaks keep score. No coins, no scroll.

Strip the coins away and the engine is: block the apps, verify a real habit, hand the time back as a reward. That engine exists on Android.
