Delete your Veto account & data

App: Veto · Developer: Shay Gavan (Veto) · Contact: vetohabits@gmail.com

You can permanently delete your Veto account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways:

Option 1 — In the app (instant)

  1. Open Veto.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon, top of the Today tab).
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account and data are removed immediately.

Option 2 — By email

If you can't access the app, email vetohabits@gmail.com from the email address on your account with the subject "Delete my account". We'll verify and delete your data within 30 days (typically much sooner).

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your account (email / Google sign-in identity)
  • Your habits, daily completions, and streaks
  • Your blocked-apps list and app settings

Delete some data without deleting your account

You don't have to close your account to remove data:

  • In the app: delete an individual habit (this also removes its completion history), remove apps from your blocked list, or change any setting — your account stays active.
  • By email: email vetohabits@gmail.com from your account address describing the data you'd like deleted, and we'll remove it while keeping your account.

What may be retained

  • Photos used for AI habit verification are never stored — they are processed for the check and discarded, so there is nothing to delete.
  • Crash diagnostics (anonymous, no habit content) may be retained for a limited period by our providers (Google Firebase) before automatic deletion.
  • Purchase records required for tax/accounting are held by Google Play / our payments provider per their retention policies; these are not used to identify you in Veto after deletion.
