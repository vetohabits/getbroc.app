Delete your account

This page explains how to delete your Broc account (the app was previously published as Veto; the steps are the same).

Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open Broc.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Under your account, choose Delete account and confirm.

Deletion is immediate. It removes your account and the data linked to it: your habits, daily completions and streaks, blocked-apps list, and settings.

Delete by email (no app needed)

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot sign in, email developer@getbroc.app from the email address on your account with the subject "Delete my Broc account". We will delete the account and confirm by reply within 30 days (usually much sooner).

What is deleted, and what is kept

  • Deleted: your account, habits, completions, streaks, blocked-apps list, and settings.
  • Kept for a limited period: crash reports and aggregate diagnostics held by our providers, which do not contain your habit content. Subscription billing records are retained by Google Play as required; manage or cancel a subscription in Google Play → Subscriptions (deleting your account does not cancel an active subscription).

Details of what we collect and why are in the privacy policy.
